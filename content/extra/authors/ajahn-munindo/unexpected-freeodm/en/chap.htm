Chapters

1. Profoundly Simple
– simplicity, gratitude to teachers, determination, effort to remember, valuing insight, gentleness

2. We are all Translators
– communal task of translating, discerning essence, respect for tradition, creative involvement, source and goal oriented practice

3. More than our Feelings
– cultivating mindfulness, middle way, right detachment, learning from everything, spontaneity and confidence

4. Getting to Know our Emotional Household
– uncovering denied life, encountering ourselves, raw emotions, feeling empty, letting go of gilt, fearlessness

5. When We Fall in Love
– capacity to be loving, absence of fear, undivided attention, pain of separateness, transformative power, intimacy

6. No Blame
– capacity to hold anger, motivation, arising of good and bad, accommodating pain, right preparation

7. Meeting our Anger
– restraining initial reactions, investigating assumptions, five degrees of distraction, conscious endurance, forgiveness

8. Who says it’s Wrong to Feel Afraid
– questioning wrong-making, listening deeply, feeding on praise and blame, false energy, realising a new identity in awareness

9. The Power of Paradox
– judgment-free awareness, multidimensional mind, compulsive judging, polluted awareness, not taking sides, relative forms

10. Truly Comfortable
– finding comfort, friendship, sanctuary, ritual, storehouse of goodness, restraining sexual desire, frustration of preferences

11. What is Renunciation
– reclaiming our energy, nothing extra, just ‘do it’, not over-doing it, not morality, letting go of ego

12. Prayer and Devotion
– what is prayer?, caught not taught, giving direction to life, fearless prayer, insight practice, accumulating potential

13 A Question of Identity
–– investigation of self, natural and unnatural identity, daily-life investigation, abiding as awareness, at-oneness, anatta

14 Alone Together
– monastic and lay lifestyle, encountering loneliness, learning from pain, living in relationship, healthy uncertainty

15 And I Know I Should Let Go
– allowing, not doing, we have the authority, self-doubt, loss of identity, fear of insanity, unexpected release

16 Contemplating Happiness
– real happiness, cultivating good companionship, wise frugality, discovering contentment as we approach death, real refuge
